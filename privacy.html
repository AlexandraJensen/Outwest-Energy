Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: February 2026

Outwest Energy Inc. is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with PIPEDA and applicable Canadian privacy legislation.

1. Information We Collect

Personal identification information submitted through contact forms; professional information in applications; technical data (IP, browser type); and communication records.

2. How We Use It

To respond to inquiries, evaluate partnerships, process applications, improve our website, comply with legal obligations, and send relevant updates where opted in.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your information. We may share with trusted service providers, professional advisors, when required by law, or in connection with a corporate transaction.

4. Security & Retention

We implement appropriate measures to protect your information. Contact inquiries retained two years; applications one year; investor communications for the relationship duration.

5. Your Rights

Access, correct, withdraw consent, request deletion, or file a complaint with the Privacy Commissioner. Contact: info@outwestenergy.com
